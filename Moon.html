Moon

Size: The Moon is about a quarter of the size of Earth, with a diameter of 3,476 km.
Orbit: The Moon orbits Earth in an elliptical path that takes 27.3 days to complete.
Appearance: The Moon's shape appears to change each night as the Sun lights up different parts of its surface.
Rotation: The Moon rotates at the same rate that it revolves around Earth.
